WHAT WE DO

Runners provides errand services for busy Suva workers who are struggling to manage their personal lives while keeping things covered in the office. We understand you cannot be in two places at once, so we’re ready to be there for you.


What can a Runner do for you?

  • Walk your dog
  • Lodge applications
  • Hold your place in line at the bank
  • Pick up your lunch
  • Do your grocery shopping (download list template)
  • Turn your lights on at home
  • Renew your drivers license
  • Deliver a package
  • Fill your prescription
  • Take your car for Certificate of Fitness
  • Do a Titles search
  • House-sit
  • Get your car washed
  • Take your TV for repair
  • Buy your spouse a present
  • Help you move house
  • Help out at parties
  • Pick up your laundry
  • Is there anything else you can think of ? tell us!

WHO WE ARE

Runners is owned by three local Suva workers, just like you, who just didn’t have the time to stand in long lines to get personnel errands done while holding down a full time job.
